Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you deliver to?

We deliver across the UAE, covering
Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Fujairah,
Ajman, Umm Al Quwain, Ras Al
Khaimah, Al Ain and Al Ghadeer.

How does your service work?

Hello Chef is a weekly meal-kit
subscription service, delivering pre-
portioned ingredients and easy-to-
follow recipes to your door. We make
home cooking simple!

What exactly do I get?

You can order 3, 4 or 5 recipes per
week to be delivered in your Hello Chef
box, with servings for 2, 3 or 4 people.
This means that you could have up to
20 servings per order!
